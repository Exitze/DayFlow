## English Metadata

### Subtitle

Shift planner and daily rhythm

### Promotional Text

Plan activities, habits, shifts, reminders, widgets, notes, and progress without accounts or tracking.

### Description

Dayflow is a local-first planner for real daily rhythm: activities, habits, shift schedules, notes, reminders, widgets, and personal statistics in one focused iPhone app.

Build a day around what actually shapes it: work shifts, training, sleep, recovery, focus, recurring routines, and personal tasks.

Dayflow helps you:

- plan today's activities and keep them tied to the correct calendar day;
- add tasks quickly with templates and selected-date planning;
- repeat activities daily, by weekdays, selected dates, or shift type;
- generate shift schedules such as 2/2, 5/2, day/night/recovery/rest, and custom patterns;
- track weekly and monthly progress, habits, categories, shifts, overtime, and payroll estimates;
- use native widgets to see progress, next actions, and shift status from the Home Screen;
- schedule local reminders for morning plans, activities, shifts, and evening review.

Dayflow is built privacy-first. The current version works without accounts, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking, or server-side storage. Your planner data stays on your device.

### Keywords

planner,calendar,habits,shift,work schedule,tasks,routine,widgets,reminders,productivity

### Review Notes

Dayflow does not require login or account creation. The app stores planner data locally using UserDefaults and an App Group shared with the WidgetKit extension.

Local notifications are optional and scheduled on device through Apple UserNotifications. The app does not use remote push notifications, advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, tracking, or server-side storage.

To test the core flow:

1. Open the app and complete onboarding.
2. Add activities from the Home screen quick add.
3. Open Calendar and create a shift schedule.
4. Open Statistics to view activity, habit, shift, and payroll summaries.
5. Add Dayflow widgets from the iOS widget gallery.
6. Open Settings to view Privacy Policy, Terms, Support, notification preferences, and local data reset controls.

Support contact: `Exitze@icloud.com`
